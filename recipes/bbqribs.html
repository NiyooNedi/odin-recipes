Baby Back Ribs

Home Page

Description

This is a seven step recipe to making the delicious baby back ribs shown above! This recipe is easily disgestible and straightforward.

Ingredients

  • Ribs 3 lbs for 6 servings
  • Water 1/2 cup
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all indgredients
  2. Season the ribs with salt and pepper
  3. Pour 1/2 cup of water into the slow cooker, and add the ribs. Scatter onion and garlic on the top. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours
  4. When the ribs are almost done, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  5. Transfer the ribs from the slow cooker to a baking sheet, and coat them with barbecue sauce
  6. Bake the ribs in the preheated oven until the sauce carmelizes and sticks to the meat; 10-15 minutes
  7. Eat!
